# Origin

Partner Graph exists after the scorecards, board brief, diligence pack, vertical brief, governance registry, deal radar, and timing signal index.

The goal is to turn that executive-intelligence estate into one named relationship map:

- which buyer, platform, and company adjacencies are strongest now
- which clusters compose into a cleaner board or investor story
- where modeled expansion value is highest
- what leadership should expand now, deepen next, or keep on watch
